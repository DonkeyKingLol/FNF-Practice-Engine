# 🎤 FNF Practice Engine - Ultimate Edition 🎹

Welcome to the **FNF Practice Engine**! Whether you're a rhythm game pro or just starting out, this tool is designed to help you master your timing and hit those "Sick!!" notes.

### 🎮 How to Play
The goal is simple: match the falling arrows with the target markers!

1.  **Watch the Arrows:** Depending on your settings, arrows will scroll **up** or **down** the screen.
2.  **Hit the Beat:** When an arrow overlaps with a target box, press the matching key on your keyboard.
3.  **Keep the Rhythm:** 
    *   **Sick!!:** Perfect timing! Gives you the most points and health.
    *   **Good:** Solid hit.
    *   **Bad:** You hit it, but the timing was off.
4.  **Watch Your Health:** Every miss drains your health bar. If it hits zero, it's Game Over!

### ⚙️ Customizing Your Experience
Click the **Settings** button to make the game your own:
*   **Keybinds:** Don't like the arrow keys? Change them to `WASD`, `DFJK`, or whatever feels best.
*   **Scroll Direction:** Switch between **Upscroll** and **Downscroll** to match your playstyle.
*   **Speed:** Want a challenge? Increase the note speed by **0.5x** increments.
*   **Offset:** If the arrows feel slightly out of sync with your presses, use the Offset tester to fix it!

### 💾 Saving
Once you've changed your settings, just hit **[BACKSPACE]** to save your changes and restart the game instantly!
